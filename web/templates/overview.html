Ideal prompt

Use LLMNoteTube to find the 25 latest trending videos on [topic], send them to NotebookLM, summarize the strongest findings, and generate a handwritten infographic.

Why it works

  • YouTube research works without any site login.
  • You control when your NotebookLM session is connected.
  • The workspace makes research, synthesis, and outputs feel like one flow.

Connection model

Browser-based NotebookLM session

LLMNoteTube does not require a Google account for site access. Connect NotebookLM only when you want analysis and artifact generation.
