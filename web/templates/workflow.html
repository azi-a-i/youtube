How it works

The LLMNoteTube workflow is designed to move from topic to deliverable without losing context.

The product is intentionally split into three stages so users can see what is ready, what still needs setup, and where they are in the research journey.

Stage 1

Login and verify readiness

Users authenticate with Google to unlock the workspace and immediately see whether research and NotebookLM services are online.

Stage 2

Search and curate sources

Teams enter a topic, review structured video results, and select the exact URLs worth sending into the notebook.

Stage 3

Analyze and generate outputs

NotebookLM receives the chosen sources, produces synthesis, and can generate artifacts once the backend connection is ready.

User experience

Overview introduces the product, Workflow explains the path, and Workspace is where authenticated users actually run the pipeline.

Important backend note

Google sign-in controls access to the site, while NotebookLM still depends on the backend having its own valid NotebookLM connection.
